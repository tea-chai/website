Introduction


Kamil Erdayandı is a Faculty Member in the Computer Engineering Department at Marmara University.

He obtained his Ph.D. in Computer Science from The University of Manchester, under the supervision of Dr. Mustafa A. Mustafa. His thesis was titled "Privacy-Preserving Trading in Local Energy Markets". He then worked as a Research Associate on the EnnCore Project, within the Systems and Software Security research group at The University of Manchester.

His research interests include Data Privacy, Applied Cryptography, Multiagent Systems, Energy Trading, Federated Learning and Blockchain.
